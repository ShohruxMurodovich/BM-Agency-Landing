## üöÄ Steps After Deployment

### 1. Replace Domain URLs
After you buy your domain, replace `https://yourdomain.com` in these files:
- [ ] `index.html` (canonical link, Open Graph, Twitter, Structured Data)
- [ ] `public/robots.txt` (Sitemap URL)
- [ ] `public/sitemap.xml` (all URLs)

### 2. Google Search Console Setup
1. Go to https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Add your website
3. Verify ownership (use HTML tag method or DNS)
4. Submit your sitemap: `https://yourdomain.com/sitemap.xml`
5. Request indexing for your homepage

### 3. Google Business Profile (Optional but Recommended)
1. Create at https://business.google.com
2. Add your business location (if physical)
3. Add photos, hours, contact info
4. Link to your website

### 4. Google Analytics (Track Visitors)
1. Go to https://analytics.google.com
2. Create a property for your website
3. Get tracking code
4. Add to your website (in index.html before closing </head>)

### 5. Social Media Integration
Create social media profiles and link them:
- Facebook Business Page
- Instagram Business
- LinkedIn Company Page
- Link these in your ContactSection component

## üÜò Common Issues & Solutions

**"Site not appearing in Google"**

- Check Google Search Console for indexing issues

- Request indexing manually in Search Console

**"Low ranking"**
- Improve content quality and length
